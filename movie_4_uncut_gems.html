Uncut Gems


Release Date: December 14, 2019
Studio: A24
Genre: Thriller
Run time: 2hr 10min
Rated: R

A jewelry dealer to the rich and famous must find a way to pay his debts when his merchandise is taken from one of his top sellers and girlfriend.

Reviews

Lily Haynes: Take Good Time and turn the production value up by a lot. Seeing the opening shot in a Safdie brothers’ movie that wasn’t New York put me in disbelief for a second. There are great performances out of everyone involved (yes, even Adam Sandler, you gotta trust me on this one). People even play themselves really well! The Weeknd was very believable as a douche and Kevin Garrnett has Oscar buzz on his side now.
